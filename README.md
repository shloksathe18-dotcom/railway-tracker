## ğŸ“± How to Use

### Live Train Tracking

1. Click on **"Live Tracking"** from the home screen
2. Choose between:
   - **Online Tracking**: Enter train number for real-time status
   - **Offline Tracking**: Enter train number and last known station
3. View current location, delays, and estimated arrival times

### PNR Status Check

1. Click on **"PNR Status"** from the home screen
2. Enter your 10-digit PNR number
3. View booking status, seat allocation, and journey details

### Train Schedule

1. Click on **"Train Schedule"** from the home screen
2. Enter the train number
3. View complete station-wise schedule with timings and platforms

### Route & Station Info

1. Click on **"Route & Station"** from the home screen
2. Choose between:
   - **Train Route**: Enter train number to view complete route
   - **Station Info**: Enter station code to view trains at that station
